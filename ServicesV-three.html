Search Engine Optimisation (SEO)

If you successfully improve the visibility of your online content, you’ll be rewarded by showing up high on a search engine’s unpaid, organic results. That’s the beauty of Search Engine Optimisation (SEO).

Our job is to remain up-to-date with Google’s algorithm changes so that we can rank you for keywords your customers are using to find your products or services online. We’re a ‘white hat’ company which means that we only use methods that adhere to Google best practices to put you at the top, and make sure you stay there too.

    What you will achieve with an ethical SEO strategy:
  • Increase your brand’s authority and credibility online
  • Rank higher for high-intent and relevant keywords
  • Gain free organic traffic from search engines
  • Increase your potential for high returns
SEO PROCESS

SEO is one of the fastest changing marketing tools around. Google is constantly adjusting and improving their best practices, keeping us on our toes as we adapt to new ways of getting you results. Our process is comprehensive and tried and tested for your peace of mind.

SEO Audit

We conduct a thorough SEO audit to see what on-page and off-page SEO factors need to be improved to enhance the searchability of your website. We look at the efforts you’re already making to optimise your content and consider how we can make them more effective with an improved strategy.

Define Target Keywords

Our SEO team uses various SEO tools such as Google’s Keyword Planner and Webmaster Tools to find the most targeted keywords for your brand’s most important products or services. We use useful long-tail, high search intent keywords to ensure we locate users who are using these terms to find you online.

On-Page & Off-Page Optimisation

We incorporate on-page and off-page SEO features to give your website the best chance of ranking on Google’s first page. We create an internal blog for your website where we incorporate searchable keywords to make you more visible via a search engine.

Monitor, Track, Optimise

We’re on top of Google’s algorithm changes and on the lookout for new keywords to help you expand your website’s reach. We regularly monitor our efforts, track your results, optimise keywords and other SEO features to create a refined, user-friendly experience on your website
